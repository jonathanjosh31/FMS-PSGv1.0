A Dual Mode system.

General Mode

The general visitor mode is for the non-registered users occasionally visiting the premises. The later mode is for the faculties and the students of that institution. No records or details are collected in the general visitor mode. In this mode, the visitor is checked for their temperature and a respective message is displayed. .

Institutional Mode

In the institutional mode, users are already registered to the system and are given with a unique QR code. This unique QR code is then scanned at the device, and then the temperature check is carried on. The temperature readings are then recorded in the database for further analysis.
