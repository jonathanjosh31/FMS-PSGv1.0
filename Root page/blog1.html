What can I do to protect myself and prevent the spread of disease?

Protective Measures against COVID-19

Protection measures for everyone

You can reduce your chances of being infected or spreading COVID19 by taking some simple precautions:

  1. Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.
  2. Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
  3. Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.
  4. Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.
  5. Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.
  6. Stay home if you feel unwell.
  7. If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention.

Staying Healthy at Home: Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is very important during the COVID-19 pandemic. What we eat and drink can affect our body’s ability to prevent, fight and recover from infections.


While no foods or dietary supplements can prevent or cure COVID-19 infection, healthy diets are important for supporting immune systems. Good nutrition can also reduce the likelihood of developing other health problems, including obesity, heart disease, diabetes and some types of cancer.

  1. Eat a variety of food, including fruits and vegetables
  2. Limit the intake of salt
  3. Eat moderate amounts of fats and oils – substitute butter, ghee and lard with healthier fats like olive, soy or sunflower oil.
  4. Limit the intake of sugar
  5. Stay hydrated by drinking enough water – drink atleast 8 glasses of water everyday
  6. Avoid hazardous and harmful alcohol use
