How It Works?

Contactless COVID-19 Screening System!!

This temperature check tablet is installed to ensure that people entering the college premises are not running a high temperature. It provides alerts when a person runs a high temperature and thus should not enter the premises. First, the user is supposed to scan their unique QR code in the device. Later, their body temperature is scanned and the output is displayed along with an alert message when the user runs high temperature.
